About Department Of Computer Application

The Department of Computer Science and Applications is a progressive and technology-driven academic unit committed to delivering high-quality education, practical skills, and research opportunities in the field of computer science. The department offers a range of specialized programs tailored to meet the growing demand for IT professionals, including the Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA), BCA in Cloud Computing, BCA in Cyber Security, and the Master of Computer Applications (MCA). These programs are designed to provide students with a strong foundation in core computing principles while also offering advanced knowledge in high-demand areas such as artificial intelligence, data analytics, cloud infrastructure, network security, and emerging digital technologies. The curriculum is regularly updated to align with current industry trends and is delivered through a mix of theoretical instruction, hands-on lab work, live projects, and internships. Our faculty members are a blend of seasoned academicians and industry experts who bring deep subject knowledge and real-world experience to the classroom. They are actively involved in research and mentorship, guiding students in academic projects, innovation challenges, and professional development. The department is equipped with modern laboratories and computing facilities to foster an environment of active learning and experimentation. We also maintain strong collaborations with industry partners to provide students with exposure to current tools, platforms, and workplace practices, enhancing their employability and career readiness. Many of our alumni hold positions in top IT firms, government agencies, and startups, reflecting the strong academic foundation and industry relevance of our programs. In addition to academics, students are encouraged to participate in coding competitions, tech fests, innovation cells, and community outreach activities to build leadership and soft skills. The department is committed to nurturing technically skilled, socially responsible, and industry-ready graduates prepared to thrive in a rapidly evolving digital world.

HOD's Message

Welcome to the Faculty of Computer Science & Applications, Mandsaur University, Mandsaur.

Education is evolving at a faster pace than any other period in recent history.  The curriculum is aligned as per the industry’s requirement to meet tomorrow’s challenges for the budding engineers and technocrats.  Our dedicated team of faculty members are skilled to solve tough problems, collaborate effectively, and express ideas in innovative ways. Various MoUs have been established with the industry. Our curriculum focuses on practical exposure, industry-aligned syllabus, problem-solving, and coding. Our curriculum is designed to prepare students to  face future prospects.

The Faculty of Computer Science & Applications comprises of four departments – BCA,BCA(Cloud Computing),BCA(Cyber Security and System Administration). Each department offers undergraduate, postgraduate & Ph.D. programs and is also running different domain-specific specialized programs to fulfill the requirements of skilled professionals as per market demand.

Our Teaching Learning Process includes activity based learning, workshops, certification courses from industry leading certification authorities. We use Google classroom for the learning management system. In Google Classroom, teachers have uploaded more than 1000 videos and study materials for the students.  

Every department has identified their Thrust areas like Artificial Intelligence, IOT, Electric Vehicle, Structure and Robotics and Automation. We conduct various activities throughout the year aligned with the thrust area.

Training and Placement Cell and faculty members from academics guide our students to choose their  career as per their aspirations. More than 80 companies visit our campus every year from various sectors to recruit our industry-ready students 

Dr. Nilesh Jain,
